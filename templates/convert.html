The minimum trade amount is generally an equivalent value of 10 USDT worth of coins. Minimum and maximum trade amounts will be dependent on the coin / pair and can be previewed before entering in a trade amount.

Trades are settled directly to your Binance account’s spot wallet. Settlement will usually occur immediately after you confirm a trade.

There is no separate wallet for OTC trades. Simply make deposits and withdrawals to / from your account’s spot wallet.

We do not charge any fees on OTC trades. However, there will be a spread (if you are buying, our prices will be above the market price. If you are selling, our price will be below the market price).

To confirm an OTC trade, you must have the required amount of coins that you are selling in your account. For example, if you want to buy 10 BTC with USDT and the price per BTC is 10,000 USDT, then you must have at least 100,000 USDT in your account to confirm the trade.
